About Little Things Speech and Language

Welcome to Little Things Speech and Language, where we believe every child deserves the opportunity to communicate confidently. Our practice offers individualized therapy tailored to meet each child where they are, creating a supportive and engaging environment for development. We are currently accepting new clients for private-pay sessions, providing flexible options for in-home or community-based appointments—whether at a library, park, or other familiar setting that best suits your child’s needs. Please note that we are not accepting insurance at this time. To learn more or to inquire about starting services, complete our contact form with a brief summary of your child's needs. We look forward to the opportunity to support your family’s communication journey with personalized care and dedication.
